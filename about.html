About

The BT Business Enterprise Day event is run by BT volunteers and is held in our main Exhibition Hall at Adastral Park (Martlesham Heath). Aimed at around 250 Year 9 or Year 10 students of mixed ability, you are grouped up into around 25 individual businesses who compete to be the day’s most successful company.

Winning the event is quite simple, you simply have to meet these milestones:

  • End the day with the most cash in the bank, and owning a large percentage of your company!
  • Build and sell virtual cars; achieved through the accumulation of car-part tokens that can only be completed by trading/negotiating with other teams.
  • Raise initial capital (for your car parts) with a start-up loan.
  • Raise initial and ongoing capital by trading in shares using our online share-trading software.
  • Maintain your company bank account using our online banking software.
  • Raise additional capital by "investing in" (paying for) and completing physical team activities and a variety of reasonably complex quizzes and questionnaires, which all provide a "return on investment".
  • Complete a mock-government paperwork exercise.
  • Have two people pitch a short two minute Dragon’s Den style presentation on "why their company is investable".
  • Negotiate with "The Union" when your "car factory workers" go on strike, trying to reach a low-cost outcome.
  • Generally manage your company/team (lead by a Chief Executive Officer and a Chief Financial Officer) and handle/negotiate with all other competing.

Ultimately to win, you’ll need to be organised and effectively build a “virtual factory” that produces many cars at a great price.
