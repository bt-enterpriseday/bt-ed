    Work Experience

    BT offers work experience for a wide range of ages starting from year 10. This opportunity provides excellent experience to those who are exploring the working world, those who are considering BT's apprenticeship scheme or maybe the people who would just like to see what BT's working enviroment is like.

    One can apply for a specific department of BT to work in and will then be put in a group with other individuals which will be assigned a host who will introduce them and guide them through their work experience. The host is a long-term BT employee who specialises in the particular field. You will also be taken on tours around the adastral park and will be shown presentations to learn more about what BT does.
