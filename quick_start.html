Quick Start Guide

About Your Companies

By now you should have been allocated to a company by your school. You should sort out your company into a variety of roles including:
Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
Finance Director
Technology Director
Marketing Director
Security Officer

What is your company goal?

Your company is out to be the best on the day. To do this you must have achieved the following by the time we close:
Have the most money in the bank (adjusted for share ownership).
Have made at least one car (more cars = more money).
Complete the bureaucratic IDOT paperwork.
Complete the end of day financial audit (all companies get audited).
